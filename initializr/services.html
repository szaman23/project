SERVICES

We offer a variety of packages to assist with your planning needs. Please contact kari@karilywood.com to book an appointment and discuss further.

  • Full Wedding Planning
  • Partial Wedding Planning which includes our Day of Wedding Coordination
  • Day of Wedding Coordination
  • *Out of Town Wedding Planning and Coordination
  • *Destination Wedding Planning

Full Service Planning

  • Budget planning and management with updates monthly
  • Assist with your wedding theme, décor and look and feel
  • Venue and ceremony recommendations
  • Vendor referrals
  • Main point of contact and communication for your vendors
  • Attend all venue and vendor meetings with follow up notes within 48 hours
  • Review all vendor contracts
  • Create a payment schedule and send friendly reminders when due
  • Attend rehearsal and assist with on-site coordination
  • Create a detailed itinerary for all vendors based on your approval
  • Oversee ceremony and reception setup, load in and assist with any special requests
  • Distribution of final payments and gratuities day-of
  • Access to our “Wedding Emergency Kit”

Partial Wedding Planning

  • Personalized to do timeline
  • Vendor recommendations based on budget and style
  • Monthly email check-ins to assist with planning and steps that must be taken
  • Unlimited communication via email and phone calls
  • Attend venue meeting(s)
  • Create and share your itinerary for all vendors
  • We will be the main point of contact for all communication 14 days prior to your wedding day!
  • Day of Wedding Coordination – See below for full description
  • Attend your rehearsal and assist with on-site coordination
  • Distribution of final payments and gratuities day-of
  • Access to our “Wedding Emergency Kit”

Day of Wedding Coordination

  • Review your current to do list
  • Create a detailed itinerary for your wedding day
  • Communicate with your vendors and share your itinerary and any special instructions
  • We will be the main point of contact for all communication 14 days prior to your wedding day!
  • Attend your final venue meeting
  • Unlimited communication via email and phone calls
  • Attend your rehearsal and assist with on-site coordination
  • Coordination of ceremony and reception details with vendors involved. Kari Lywood Events will set up the following items:
    1. Menu cards*
    2. Table numbers*
    3. Escort cards or seat chart*
    4. Wedding favours*
  • Main point of contact on your wedding day to ensure all is set and running smoothly
  • Distribution of final vendor payments and gratuities day-of
  • Access to our “Wedding Emergency Kit”

*Please note all items must be labeled and at the venue with any specific instructions.
